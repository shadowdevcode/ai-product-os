# Issue 001

## Idea
AI PM Portfolio Generator

## Problem
Product managers struggle to showcase structured proof-of-work projects.

Most portfolios are static and fail to demonstrate product thinking, experiments, and metrics.

## User
Early career product managers
APM candidates
PMs transitioning into AI roles

## Opportunity
Create a tool that converts PRDs, case studies, and experiment logs into a structured PM portfolio automatically.

## Hypothesis
If PMs can convert their product work into portfolio-ready case studies automatically, they will adopt the tool for job search preparation.

## Success Metric
Number of portfolios generated

Secondary metrics

Portfolio share rate
Portfolio completion rate
